About Us

Introduction

We take this opportunity to introduce our company AMK Engineering & Construction Company having expertise in construction of rigid and flexible pavements for roads / highways with construction materials, reinforced earth and earthwork. We have knowledge in real estate, industrial construction and steel trading, equipment and workforce to plan, design and execute any road construction contract. In addition, the company also deals in the work of Water Drainage System, RCC (Reinforced Cement Concrete) Box Drain, and Ready-mix Concrete Road Construction apart from cold milling of pavements, INCA Projects etc. We have successfully satisfied a list of clients by executing projects within the set time period and are dedicated to serve the specific need of business involved in construction industry. Our clientele include various reputed concerns belonging to Government and Semi-Government, as well as private sector. The company undertakes developments of various infrastructure projects in the road and pavement building sector. We have a team of highly qualified and experienced professionals, award winning architects and well trained staffs are equally committed to the Company’s objective.The company possesses sufficient resources to execute and complete the undertaken construction projects within the stipulated time period. Our list of resources is vast and all-inclusive and is also flexible enough to meet the increased and sophisticated requirements of our clients. All our resources can be categorized as follows:

  • Machines and equipment’s.
  • Shuttering/scaffolding materials.
  • Specialized labour force (skilled/ unskilled).
  • Cadre of loyal and faithful suppliers.
  • Industrial Infrastructure.
  • On time project execution.

Our Strength

Our company foundation is built on a strong commitment to Quality, Safety, Innovation, Flexibility and Customer Satisfaction. We specialize in all aspects of Commercial paving that includes both large and smaller contracts. Our experience and expertise enables us to provide excellent results in a timely manner. We have gained strength in our field while doing projects for Chennai Airport.Our experienced staff of contractor, engineers, planners, supervisors, material suppliers, amply supported by a large seasoned labour force, has the knowledge and ability to plan, design, supervise and construct as per the client's requirements. In addition to our facilities and workforce, continuous research activities assist us substantially in serving our customers using up-to-date techniques and equipment. Our manpower is our asset and we lay complete concern about their safety and security.
